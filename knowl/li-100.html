if \(\lambda_0>u(x_0)\text{,}\) then \(\xi\not\in\pa\Omega\text{,}\) otherwise

\begin{equation*} u(\xi)>u(x_0)+p\cdot (\xi-x_0)\geq 0 \end{equation*}

contradicting to \(u\leq 0\) on \(\pa\Omega\text{.}\) Since we have

\begin{equation*} u(\xi)+p\cdot (x-\xi)\geq u(x), \forall x\in\bar\Omega\text{,} \end{equation*}

where \(\xi\in\Gamma_u^+\) and \(p\in\chi(\Gamma_u^+)\text{.}\)
