Let the coefficients of \(L\) satisfy the uniform elliptic condition. \(u\in W^{2,n}(\Omega)\) satisfies \(Lu=f\text{.}\) Let \(f/\lambda\in L^n(\Omega)\text{.}\) Suppose \(u\geq 0\) on \(B_{2R}(y)\subset\Omega\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} \sup_{B_{\frac R2}(y)}\, u\leq C\left(\inf_{B_{\frac R2}(y)}\, u+R\left|\left|\frac f\lambda\right|\right|_{L^n(B_{2R}(y)}\right)\text{.} \end{equation*}
