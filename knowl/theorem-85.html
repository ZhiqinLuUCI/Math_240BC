Theorem 6.5.4.

Let \(\mathcal H\) be a Hilbert space and let \(A: \mathcal H\to\mathcal H\) be a compact operator. Then the essential spectrum \(\sigma_{ ess}(A)\subset\{0\}\text{.}\) Moreover, if \(\sigma_{ ess}(A)=\emptyset\text{,}\) then \(\mathcal H\) is the direct sum of eigenspaces of each non-zero eigenvalues, that is, if \(E_m\) is the eigenspaces of the eigenvalue \(\lambda_m\text{.}\) Then we have

\begin{equation*} \mathcal H=\bigoplus_m E_m\text{.} \end{equation*}
