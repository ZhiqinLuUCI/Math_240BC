By the Riemann-Lebesgue lemma, we have

\begin{equation*} \left|\int_M F(x,\xi) e^{i\langle x,(\xi-\tilde\xi)\rangle}\right|\leq C\frac{1+\|\xi\|^2}{\|\xi-\tilde\xi\|^K} \end{equation*}

for any \(K>0\text{.}\) Below we shall take \(K=(s+1)/2\) and use the inequality that

\begin{equation*} (1+\|\xi\|^2)\cdot\|\xi-\tilde\xi\|\leq C\,(1+\|\tilde\xi\|^2)\text{.} \end{equation*}
