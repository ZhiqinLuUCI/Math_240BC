Then by the theorem of Cartan, there are one forms \(\{ \omega_{ij} \}\) with \(\omega_{ij} = - \omega _{ji}\text{,}\) called the connection forms, such that

\begin{equation*} \left\{ \begin{array}{ll} d \omega_i = - \omega_{ij} \wedge \omega_j \\ d \omega _{ij} = - \omega _{ik} \wedge \omega_{kj} + \frac{1}{2} R_{ij kl} \, \omega _k \wedge \omega _l \end{array} \right.\text{.} \end{equation*}
