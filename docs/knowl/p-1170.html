Let \(p\in M\) be a fixed point. Let \(\rho\) be the distance function with respect to \(p\text{,}\) Let \(R>0\) be a large number. Fixing \(x_0\in \partial B_R(p)\text{.}\) By the Laplacian comparison theorem, we have

\begin{equation*} \Delta\rho^2 \leqslant 2n\text{.} \end{equation*}
