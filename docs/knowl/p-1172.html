We choose a standard cut-off function \(\phi=\psi(\rho(x))\text{,}\) where

\begin{equation*} \psi(t) = \left\{\begin{array}{lcl} 1 \amp \amp 0 \leqslant t \leqslant R-1 \\ \frac{1}{2}(R+1-t) \amp \amp R-1 \leqslant t \leqslant R+1 \\ 0 \amp \amp t \geqslant R+1 \end{array} \right.\text{.} \end{equation*}
