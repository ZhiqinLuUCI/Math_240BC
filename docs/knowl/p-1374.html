For \(\mathbb R^n\text{,}\) the fundamental solution of the heat equation

\begin{equation*} (\Delta-\frac{\pa}{\pa t}) u=0 \end{equation*}

is

\begin{equation*} \frac{1}{(4\pi t)^{n/2}}e^{-\frac{r^2}{4t}}\text{,} \end{equation*}

where \(r=d(x,y)\) is the Euclidean distance of \(x\) and \(y\text{.}\)
