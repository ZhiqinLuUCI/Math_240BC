Theorem 2.3.2.

We have

\begin{equation} \lambda_k = \inf \left\{ \left. \frac{ \int_{\Omega} |\nabla f |^2 }{\int_{\Omega} f^2 } \, \right| \, \, \, \left. f\right|_{\pa \Omega} = 0, \, f \not\equiv 0 = \int_{\Omega} f \phi_j, \, \, \forall \, j \lt k \right\}\text{,}\tag{2.3.1} \end{equation}

where \(\phi_j\) are the eigenfunctions of \(\lambda_j\text{.}\)
