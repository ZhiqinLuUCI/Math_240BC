Lemma 8.2.7.

Let \(u\in \mathcal C(\Omega)\text{.}\) Then

  1. for any \(y\in\Gamma_u\text{,}\)

    \begin{equation*} |p|\leq\frac{2\sup|u|}{{ dist}(y,\pa\Omega)},\qquad \forall p\in\chi(y)\text{,} \end{equation*}

  2. the normal map maps a compact subset of \(\Omega\) to a closed set of \(\R^n\text{.}\)
