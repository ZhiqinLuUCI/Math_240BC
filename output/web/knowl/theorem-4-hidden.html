Let \(d_{\nabla^2}\) be the operator defined by the composition

\begin{equation*} TM\overset{\nabla}{\longrightarrow}TM\otimes T^*M\overset{\nabla}{\longrightarrow}TM\otimes T^*M\otimes T^*M\overset{\wedge}{\longrightarrow}TM\otimes\Lambda^2(M)\text{.} \end{equation*}

Then it is the curvature operator.
