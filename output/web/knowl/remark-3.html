Remark 3.1.5.

The proof of the laplacian comparison theorem in this section is not that far away from the usual proof using the second variational formula of the geodesic distance. The fact that we can write the Riemannian metric in the form

\begin{equation*} dr^2 + h_{ij} d \theta_i d \theta_j \end{equation*}

is ensured by the Gauss Lemma, which is proved by the first variational formula of the geodesic distance function.
