Let \(U \subset \mathbb{R} ^{n-1}\) be an open set. Let \(\eta _2 , \ldots, \eta _n\) be smooth one forms on \(U\) such that

\begin{equation*} (ds^1)^2 = \eta ^2_2 + \cdots + \eta ^2_n \end{equation*}

defines a Riemannian metric on \(U\text{.}\) Let \(\theta _2, \ldots, \theta _n\) be local coordinates of \(U\text{.}\) Let \(f (r, \theta)\) be a positive function on \(\mathbb{R}^+ \times U\text{.}\) Define a Riemannian metric

\begin{equation*} ds^2 = d r ^2 + f^2 (r, \theta) \sum ^n _{i=2} \eta ^2 _i \end{equation*}

on \(\mathbb{R} ^+ \times U\text{.}\)
