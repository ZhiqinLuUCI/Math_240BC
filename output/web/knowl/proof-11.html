Proof

Let \(\phi\in H^1(M)\text{.}\) Then there exists a sequence \(\{\phi_j\}\in \Lambda^p(M)\) such that

\begin{equation*} \phi_j\to \phi, j\to\infty \end{equation*}

in the \(\|\|_1\) norm.

Let \(p\in M\) be a fixed point and let \(\rho\) be a smooth function on \(\mathbb R\) with compact support such that in a neighborhood of \(0\text{,}\) \(\rho\) is the constant \(1\text{.}\) Let \(d(x,y)\) be the distance function. Define

\begin{equation*} \rho_j(x)=\rho(j^{-1}\,d(p,x))\text{.} \end{equation*}

Then we can prove that

\begin{equation*} \rho_j\,\phi_j\to\phi \end{equation*}

in the \(\|\|_1\) norm.
