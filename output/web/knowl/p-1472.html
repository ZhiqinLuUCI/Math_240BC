The graph of \(u\) is a cone with \((x_0,\lambda)\) as the vertex and with \(B_d(x_0)\) as the base. We have \(\Gamma_u=\Omega\) and

\begin{equation*} \chi(y)=\left\{ \begin{array}{ll} B_{\lambda/d}(0) \amp y=x_0;\\ -\frac{\lambda}{d}\cdot\frac{y-x_0}{|y-x_0|} \amp y\neq x_0. \end{array} \right. \end{equation*}
