Theorem 3.1.8.

Let \(M\) be a complete Riemannian manifold such that \(Ricci (M) \geq (n-1) k , n = \dim M\text{.}\) We use \(B (x_0, r)\) to denote the ball centered at \(x _0\) with radius \(r\text{.}\) Let \(V (k, r)\) be the ball of radius \(r\) in a simply connected space form. Then with the Dirichlet boundary condition, we have

\begin{equation*} \lambda _1 (B (x_0, r)) \leq \lambda _1 (V (k, r))\text{.} \end{equation*}
