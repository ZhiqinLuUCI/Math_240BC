Let \(\psi(r)\) be a function of \(r\text{,}\) and let \(g=\det (s_{ij})\text{.}\) Then we have

\begin{align*} \amp \Delta\psi=\frac{d^2\psi}{dr^2}+\frac nr\cdot\frac{\pa\psi}{\pa r}+\left(\frac{d\log \sqrt g}{dr}\right)\frac{d\psi}{dr},\\ \amp \Delta(\phi\psi)=\phi\Delta\psi+\psi\Delta\phi+2\frac{d\phi}{dr}\frac{d\psi}{dr}\text{.} \end{align*}
