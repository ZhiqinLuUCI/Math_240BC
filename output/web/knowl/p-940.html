As is well-known, the eigenfunctions of the sector are of the form

\begin{equation*} f(r)\sin\frac{\theta}{\alpha} \end{equation*}

where \(f(r)\) is the so-called Bessel function

\begin{equation*} f'' + \frac{1}{r}f' + \frac{1}{r^2}(r^2 - \frac{1}{\alpha^2})f = -\lambda f \end{equation*}
