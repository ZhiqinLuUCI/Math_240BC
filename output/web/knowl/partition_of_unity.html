Proposition 1.1.2.

Let \(\{U_j\}_I\) be a locally finite cover of \(M\text{.}\) Then there exists a partition of unity \(\{\rho_j\}\) subordinating to the cover \(\{U_j\}_I\text{.}\) Moreover, let

\begin{equation*} J=\{j\in I\mid U_j\cap \pa M\neq \emptyset\}\text{.} \end{equation*}

Then \(\rho_j|_{\pa M\cap U_j}\) is a partition of unity subordinating to the cover \(\{U_j\cap\pa M\}_{j\in J}\) of \(\partial M\text{.}\)
