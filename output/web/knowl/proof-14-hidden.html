Let \(\rho_j\) be the smooth function defined in TheoremÂ 2.2.3. Then we have

\begin{equation*} 0=(\bar\Delta f, \rho_j^2 f)=Q(f,\rho_j^2 f)\text{.} \end{equation*}

Thus we have

\begin{equation*} \int_M\rho_j^2|\nabla f|^2=-2\int_M \rho_j\, f\nabla\rho_j\nabla f\leq 2\sqrt{\int_M|\nabla \rho_j|^2 f^2\, dV_M} \cdot\sqrt{\int_M\rho_j^2|\nabla f|^2\, dV_M}\text{.} \end{equation*}

It follows that

\begin{equation*} \int_M\rho_j^2|\nabla f|^2\,dV_M\leq \frac{C}{j^2}\int_Mf^2\, dV_M\to 0 \end{equation*}

and the theorem is proved.
