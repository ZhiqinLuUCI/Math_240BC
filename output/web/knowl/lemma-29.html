Lemma 6.3.4.

Let \(M\) be a complete non-compact manifold with non-negative Ricci curvature. Let \(B(R)\) be a very large ball of radius \(R\text{.}\) Let \(\lambda\) be a Dirichlet eigenvalue and let \(f\) be its eigenfunction of \(B(R)\text{.}\) Then there is a constant \(C > 0\) such that

\begin{equation*} \int_{B(R)\cdot B(R-1)} f^2 \leqslant C\int_{B(R)} f^2\text{.} \end{equation*}
