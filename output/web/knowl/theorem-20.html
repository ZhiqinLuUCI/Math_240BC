Theorem 2.5.1. Co-area formula.

Let \(M\) be a compact manifold with boundary. \(f\in H^1(M)\text{.}\) Then for any nonnegative function \(g\) on \(M\text{,}\) we have

\begin{equation*} \int_M g=\int_{-\infty}^\infty\left(\int_{\{f=\sigma\}}\frac{g}{|\nabla f|}\right)d\sigma\text{.} \end{equation*}
