Let \(K_0\) be a tube in \(\R^n\) and let \(\Gamma\) be a measurable subset of \(K_0\text{.}\) Let \(0\lt \delta\lt 1\text{.}\) Let

\begin{equation*} \Gamma_\delta=\bigcup\{ K_{3R}(y)\cap K_0\mid K_R(y)\subset K_0, |\Gamma\cap K_R(y)|\geq\delta|K_R(y)|\}\text{.} \end{equation*}

If \(\Gamma_\delta\neq K_0\text{,}\) then

\begin{equation*} |\Gamma|\leq\delta|\Gamma_\delta|\text{.} \end{equation*}
