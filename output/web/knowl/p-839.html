Let \(\triangle\) be a triangle and let \(d = d (\triangle)\) be the diameter of \(\triangle\text{.}\) That is, \(d\) is the longest side of the triangle. Then \(\forall C > 0\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} ( \lambda _2 (\triangle ) - \lambda _1 (\triangle )) d^2 (\triangle ) \leq C \end{equation*}

is a compact set.
