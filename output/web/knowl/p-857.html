Since

\begin{equation*} \int _{v^{\prime}} \rho \nabla \rho f _1 \nabla f_1 = \frac{1}{2} \int _{v^{\prime}} \nabla \rho^2 \nabla f_1^2 = - \frac{1}{2} \int _{v^{\prime}} \Delta \rho^2 f_1^2 \end{equation*}

and \(\nabla \rho, \Delta \rho = 0\) on \(v\text{,}\) we must have

\begin{equation*} \int _{u^{\prime}} -\rho \Delta \rho f _1^2 - 2 \rho\nabla \rho f_1 \nabla f _1 \leq \frac{1}{d^{2 \varepsilon}} \int _{v^{\prime} - v} f _1^2 \leq \alpha ^{\frac{2}{3} - 3 \varepsilon}\text{.} \end{equation*}
