Then \(\mathfrak D(\phi,\psi)\) defines an inner product on \(\Lambda^p(M)\text{.}\) Apparently we have the inequality

\begin{equation*} \mathfrak D(\phi,\phi)\leq C||\phi||_1^2 \end{equation*}

for some constant \(C\text{.}\) The Garding inequality states that the inverse inequality is also true so that the norms \(\sqrt{\mathfrak D(\phi,\phi)}\) and \(||\phi||_1\) are equivalent.
