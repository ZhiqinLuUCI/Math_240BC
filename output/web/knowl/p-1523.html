For the sake of simplicity, we only consider the following equation

\begin{equation} Lu=-a^{ij} D_{ij} u=f\tag{9.4} \end{equation}

on \(\Omega\text{,}\) where \((a^{ij})\) satisfies the uniform ellipticity condition:

\begin{equation} \lambda>0, \Lambda/\lambda\leq\gamma\tag{9.5} \end{equation}

on \(\Omega\text{,}\) where \(\lambda, \Lambda\) are the minimum and maximum eigenvalues of \((a^{ij})\) respectively, and \(\gamma\) is a positive number.
