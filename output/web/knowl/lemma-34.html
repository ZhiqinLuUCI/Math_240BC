Lemma 8.2.4.

For any \(N\) sufficiently large, we have

  1. \(\limt u_N(x,y,t)=\delta_x(y)\text{;}\)

  2. The kernel \(R_N(x,y,t)=\left(\Delta_y-\frac{\pa}{\pa t}\right)u_N(x,y,t)\) satisfies the estimate

    \begin{equation*} ||R_N(x,y,t)||_{\mathcal C^l}\leq C(l)t^{N-l/2-1}\text{.} \end{equation*}
