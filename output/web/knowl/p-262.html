Sobolev inequality. Assume that \(M\) is a compact manifold with boundary, then there is a constant \(C\) such that

\begin{equation} C\left(\int_Mf^{\frac{n}{n-1}}\right)^{\frac{n-1}n}\leq\int_M|\nabla f|\tag{2.5.1} \end{equation}

for any smooth functions satisfies the Dirichlet or Neumann boundary conditions.
