We consider the domain \(\Omega_\varepsilon\) and let \(U_\varepsilon\) be the first Neumann eigenfunction. By what we proved in the last lecture, we have

\begin{equation*} \mu_\varepsilon \geqslant \frac{\pi^2}{d_\varepsilon^2} \end{equation*}

where \(d_\varepsilon\) is the diameter of \(\Omega_\varepsilon\text{.}\) Since \(d_\varepsilon\rightarrow d\text{,}\) the theorem is proved.
