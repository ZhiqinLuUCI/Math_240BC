We let

\begin{align*} \amp \psi=\frac{1}{(4\pi t)^{n/2}}e^{-\frac{r^2}{4t}},\\ \amp \phi=\phi_0+\phi_1 t+\cdots+\phi_Nt^N\text{,} \end{align*}

where \(\phi_j=\phi_j(x,y)\) are smooth local functions on \(M\times M\text{.}\) We assume that under our coordinate system, \(y\) is the origin 1 . Then

\begin{equation*} u_N=\psi\phi=\frac{1}{(4\pi t)^{n/2}}e^{-\frac{r^2}{4t}} \sum_{i=0}^N\phi_i t^i\text{.} \end{equation*}
