Let

\begin{equation*} X=\sum a^i\frac{\pa}{\pa x_i} \end{equation*}

be a vector field of \(M\text{.}\) The divergence of the vector field \(X\) is defined to be

\begin{equation*} { div}\, X=\sum_i\frac{\pa a^i}{\pa x^i}+\frac 12 a^i\frac{\pa}{\pa x_i}\log\det(g_{k\ell})\text{.} \end{equation*}
