Theorem 9.2.13.

Let \(u\in \mathcal \mathcal C(\bar\Omega)\cap W_{{ loc}}^{2,n}(\Omega)\) and let

\begin{equation*} -a^{ij}D_{ij} u\leq f\text{.} \end{equation*}

Then

\begin{equation*} \sup_\Omega u(x)\leq\sup_{\pa\Omega} u(x)+\frac{d}{n\sqrt[n]{w_n}}\left|\left|f^+/\mathcal D^*\right|\right|_{L^n(\Gamma_u^+)}\text{,} \end{equation*}

where \(v=u-\sup_{\pa\Omega} u(x)\text{,}\) and \(\mathcal D^*=(\det a^{ij})^{1/n}\text{.}\)
