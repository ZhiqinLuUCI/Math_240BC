We consider all \(\xi\) with \(||\xi||\leq R\text{.}\) There are only finitely many such \(\xi\)'s. By the assumption on the sequence \(\{u_k\}\text{,}\) there is an \(N>0\text{,}\) such that

\begin{equation*} \sum_{||\xi||\leq R} (1+||\xi||^2)^r |(u_k)_\xi-(u_l)_\xi|^2\lt \eps \end{equation*}

for \(k,l>N\text{.}\) Thus we have

\begin{equation*} ||u_k-u_l||_r^2\leq\eps+\sum_{||\xi||>R} (1+||\xi||^2)^r |(u_k)_\xi-(u_l)_\xi|^2\text{.} \end{equation*}
